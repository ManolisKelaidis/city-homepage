Visit Chania

Chania, Crete offers a variety of activities including exploring historic Old Town, visiting museums and galleries, enjoying the beautiful beaches and crystal-clear waters.

Top three activities to do at Chania

Explore the Old Town:

Chania's Old Town is one of the most picturesque in Greece, with narrow streets, old buildings, and beautiful architecture

Visit the Archaeological Museum of Chania:

If you're interested in history and archaeology, the Archaeological Museum of Chania is a must-see.

Relax on the beaches:

Chania is surrounded by beautiful beaches, including Balos, Elafonisi, and Falassarna.
